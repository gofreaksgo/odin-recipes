Hot Dogs

Description

The greatest of all American foods! Be sure to get the all beef kosher dogs.

Ingredients

  • Beef Hot Dog
  • Bun
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Boil or grill the hot dog until the internal temperature reaches 165 degrees.
  2. Toast the bun (optional)
  3. Put the dog on the bun.
  4. Top with mustard.
